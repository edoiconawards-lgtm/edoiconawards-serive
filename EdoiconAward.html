Preamble

The Edo Icon Award Is Borne Out Of The Passion To Celebrate Edolites That Have Contributed Immensely To The Development And Growth Of Edo State. The Edo Icon Award Objectives Is To Celebrate Edo Personalities With Style And Glamour. We Are Celebrating Edolites, That Have Distinguished Themselves In Various Field Of Human Endeavors In Terms Of Human Capital, Social Economic, Governance, Politics, Education, Banking, Sports, Infrastructure Development Over The Past 33yrs In Edo State (the Heartbeat Of Nigeria).

About Us

The Edo Icon Award is an annual recognition event meant to honour outstanding individuals from Edo State (or with Edo heritage) across public and private sectors. The award recognizes excellence in a wide range of fields governance, business, media, public service, entrepreneurship, etc. According to the organizers, recipients are selected after research into their records meaning the award aims to celebrate “value-creation” and accomplishment, not social status or connections

It recognizes individuals who have excelled and motivates them (and others) to continue impactful work.

Purpose & Significance

  1. Recognition & Motivation: It recognizes individuals who have excelled and motivates them (and others) to continue impactful work.
  2. Promoting Role Models: Especially useful in your interest area — women’s empowerment, youth development, community leadership — the Award helps highlight role models from Edo whose stories can inspire others.
  3. Preserving Heritage & Legacy: By honouring diverse categories (public service, media, entrepreneurship, etc.), it helps immortalize contributions of Edo-origin persons to state/national development.
  4. to foster the spirit of hard work in our youth to enable good standard of living.

Recent Edition: 2025 Highlight

  • The 2025 edition of the Edo Icon Award was recently held. Among the recipients: the current Governor of Edo State (Monday Okpebholo), his Deputy (Dennis Idahosa), business leader Leemon Ikpea, and media professional Ozoruvba Aliu — awarded “Edo Icon Print Media Personality of the Year”.
  • The organiser (Mackjus Consultancy) says the aim is to recognise Edo people — whether based in Edo or diaspora — whose work positively impacts Edo State or Nigeria broadly.
